Teaching Methods

Our teaching methods help kids to memorise the formulas and material we tutor them on. We use charts diagrams and even sometimes games to help a child understand what needs to be done on a certain problem. We also often use an online learning program called IXL. On IXL if they get a problem wrong the program explains to them how the problem should be solved. Try EnchantedLearning.com too. On there you can print out worksheets charts and graphs on almost every subject and topic.

Other Options

My mother is sometimes assitant teacher for when we stumble across something I dont understand. If you would prefer an adult to work with your child rather than another kid then request for my mother to tutor your child.
